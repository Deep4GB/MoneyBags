How It Works

Welcome to the explanation of our Stock Predictor! This page will detail the various steps and processes involved in the functionality of our tool.

The Stock Predictor utilizes historical stock data, machine learning algorithms, and predictive analysis to forecast future stock prices.

Our tool fetches historical stock data from Yahoo Finance, preprocesses the data, trains a machine learning model using RandomForestRegressor, and predicts future stock prices based on the learned patterns.

The prediction is based on various features such as opening price, high price, low price, volume, and moving averages of the stock data.

For a better understanding, we have also included various visualization techniques using Plotly and Matplotlib to help comprehend the process more intuitively.
